  • Modern architecture: Astana is famous for its unique modern architecture, including the symbol of the city - the Baiterek Monument and the Government Building of the Republic of Kazakhstan, which makes it incredible for lovers of architectural masterpieces.
  • Historical sites: Despite its modernity, the city also offers a rich historical heritage, including museums and monuments such as the mausoleum of Khoja Ahmed Yasawi and the Hazret Sultan Mosque.
  • Parks and green areas: Nur-Sultan has beautiful parks and embankments, such as Astana Riverside and Astana Botany, where tourists can relax and enjoy nature.
  • Natural wealth: Almaty is surrounded by the magnificent Tien Shan Mountains, which makes it an ideal place for lovers of outdoor activities, mountain hikes and winter sports.
  • Cultural heritage: The city is rich in cultural and historical attractions, including museums, monuments and architectural masterpieces such as Panfilov Park and Zenkov Cathedral
  • Exotic markets: Almaty is known for its picturesque markets where tourists can taste local products, handicrafts and national cuisine.
  • Historical monuments: Karaganda is rich in historical monuments, including the Karaganda City Museum and the monument of the Kazakh SSR.
  • Ski resorts: Near the city there are ski resorts such as "Karaganda Bekmes" and "Shakhty Mountains", which attract outdoor enthusiasts.
  • Methnographic complex "Kazakh Village": Here tourists can immerse themselves in the culture and traditions of the Kazakh people by visiting ethnographic exhibitions and master classes.
